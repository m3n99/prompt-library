## Positioning & Messaging

- **Positioning statement (final):** One choice from Section 02, refined for
  the specific launch moment.
- **Tagline:** 5–8 words. Memorable, outcome-focused.
- **Elevator pitch (30 seconds):** Write it out verbatim for the founder to
  memorize.
- **Value narrative by persona:** For each key persona — the specific pain you
  solve, the outcome they get, and the "aha moment" in the product.
- **Objection responses:** Top 5 sales objections and the exact response to each.


## Pre-Launch (T-minus 4 weeks)

### Build-in-Public Strategy
  - Platform(s): LinkedIn | Twitter/X | specific communities — which and why.
  - Content calendar: 3 posts per week — topics, format, goal of each.
  - Waitlist strategy: How to collect signups before launch. Landing page
    copy included.

### Beta Tester Recruitment
  - How many beta testers: [N] — why this number.
  - Criteria for ideal beta tester.
  - Outreach method and message (write the actual message):
    ```
    Subject: [subject line]

    [Body of the outreach message]
    ```
  - What beta testers get (early access, discount, co-design input).
  - What you get from them (feedback structure, testimonials, case studies).

### Discovery Call Script
  - Opening (2 min): How to start.
  - Discovery questions (10 min): 5–7 specific questions that uncover pain,
    budget, and buying process.
  - Demo flow (15 min): What to show and in what order.
  - Close (5 min): How to end with a clear next step.
  - Common objections and responses.

### Converting to Case Studies
  - When to ask (timing relative to first value moment).
  - What to ask for (quote, logo, written case study, video).
  - The email template to request it.
  - How to use case studies in future sales.


## Growth Channels

For each channel relevant to this product and budget:

### Channel: [e.g. Content Marketing]
  - **Tactic:** Specific execution plan (not just "write blogs").
  - **Target keyword / community / platform.**
  - **Content type and cadence.**
  - **Owner:** Who creates and publishes (founder | hire | agency).
  - **Budget:** What it costs to execute properly.
  - **Timeline to first results:** Realistic estimate.
  - **Success metric:** How to know it's working.
  - **Kill metric:** When to stop investing in this channel.

Channels to evaluate (include all relevant ones):
  - Cold outbound (email, LinkedIn)
  - Content / SEO
  - Community-led (Slack groups, Discord, Reddit, forums)
  - Product-led growth (free tier, viral loops, referrals)
  - Partnerships / integrations
  - Paid acquisition (when budget allows)
  - Events / webinars / speaking


## Partnership Activation Plan
